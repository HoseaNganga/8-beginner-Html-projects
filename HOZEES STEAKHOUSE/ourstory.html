Our Story

Over the last decade, Hozee steak has established itself as Nairobi's Favourite Steakhouse.We are honoured to serve our customers in three branches located at Viking House, Westlands, Galana Plaza, Kilimani, and Panari Sky Centre, Mombasa Rd.

Hozee steakhouse is an authentic Brazilian Steakhouse that offers the most delectable selection of charcoal grilled meats that go along with a full buffet to choose from all for a set price per person, all you can eat. This concept in Brazil is referred to as Rodizio.

The ambience in our restaurants is sure to give you the feeling that you are sitting in a Churrascaria (Steakhouse) right in the heart of Brazil as you enjoy a delicious meal for whatever occasion you are celebrating be it a birthday, anniversary, graduation, retirement, office party or just a special meal with your family and friends.
