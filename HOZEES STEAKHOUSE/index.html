Welcome to Hozee's Steakhouse

Late 2007, native Kenyan Hozee Chefs began sharing the centuries-old grilling tradition of churrasco in Nairobi.
Hozee's Steakhouse is Nairobi's only All You Can Eat Kenyan Churrascaria serving 17 cuts of meat and 25 different salads, sides and dessets.
Welcome to the Hozee's Experience at our 3 branches located in Viking House, Westlands, Galana Plaza, Kilimani, and Panari Sky Centre, Mombasa Rd.
